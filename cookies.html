Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

Home / Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2025
This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies our website uses, and how you can control them. By continuing to use scollegeschool.com, you consent to the use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit — such as your language preference or whether you have already seen a notice — making your next visit easier and the site more useful.

Cookies cannot access other files on your device and do not contain viruses or any harmful software.

2. Cookies We Use

Our website uses only the minimum cookies necessary to function correctly. We do not currently use any advertising, profiling, or analytics tracking cookies.

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
acs_session Functional Maintains your chatbot session so your conversation is not lost on page reload. Session (deleted when you close the browser)
navbar_scroll Functional Stores scroll position so the sticky navbar behaves correctly. Session

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our website embeds the ACS Chatbot widget, hosted at acs-chatbot-4oym.onrender.com. This widget may set a session cookie to maintain your chat conversation. This cookie:

  • Contains only a temporary session identifier — no personal data
  • Is deleted automatically when you close your browser
  • Is not shared with any advertising or analytics platforms

Our website also links to external social media platforms (Facebook, X/Twitter, YouTube, TikTok). If you click those links, the destination sites may set their own cookies according to their own policies, which are beyond our control.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here is how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the chatbot and certain interactive features on our website.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our website evolves. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our website after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
