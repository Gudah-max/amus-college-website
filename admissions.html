How to Apply

Simple Admission Process

Four easy steps to join the Amus College School family.

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form below or collect a physical form from our school office in Uganda.

2

Submit Documents

Provide academic transcripts, birth certificate, passport photos, and any relevant certificates from previous schools.

3

Assessment & Interview

Selected applicants will be invited for an entrance assessment and a short interview with our academic team.

4

Acceptance & Enrollment

Successful applicants receive an offer letter. Complete enrollment by paying the required fees and submitting remaining documents.

Requirements

Admission Requirements

We admit students based on academic merit, character, and potential. Below are the requirements for each level.

🎓 O-Level Admission (S1 – S4)
  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) results slip
  • Birth certificate
  • Introductory letter from previous school
  • 2 passport-size photographs
  • Minimum aggregate of 12 in PLE
🏆 A-Level Admission (S5 – S6)
  • UCE results slip with minimum of 6 passes
  • At least 2 principal passes in chosen combination subjects
  • Transfer letter from previous O-Level school
  • 2 passport-size photographs
📋 Important Notes
  • Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis
  • Fees structure available on request from the school office
  • Bursaries may be available for exceptional students
  • Contact school for boarding availability
  • Early applications are strongly encouraged

Opening Doors

Transforming Lives Through Scholarships

At Amus College School, we believe that talent and potential should never be limited by financial circumstance.

In 2024, we successfully enrolled 350 students under academic and football scholarships — an initiative that has changed hundreds of lives.

These scholarships are more than financial support. They represent our deep commitment to opening doors for deserving students to access quality education and to showcase their talents, both academically and athletically.

It has been a privilege to witness these young people thrive — knowing that we were able to provide them with the resources, mentorship, and platform they needed to excel. The lasting impact of this initiative stands as a powerful testament to the mission of Amus College School.
