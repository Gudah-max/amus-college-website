Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the new academic year begin? +

The academic year typically begins in February following the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) calendar. Applications should be submitted by January for the best placement chances.

Does Amus College offer boarding? +

Please contact our admissions office directly for the latest information on boarding availability, facilities, and fees.

What are the school fees? +

School fees vary by level (O-Level, A-Level) and whether a student is a day scholar or boarder. Please contact our office at +256782442940 for the current fees structure.

Can I visit the school before applying? +

Absolutely! We warmly welcome campus visits. Please contact us to schedule an appointment and we will arrange a guided tour with our team.

Are bursaries or scholarships available? +

We are committed to making quality education accessible. Limited bursary support may be available for academically exceptional students with demonstrated financial need. Contact our office to inquire.
