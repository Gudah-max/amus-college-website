Our Identity

Built on a Legacy of Light

Amus College School is a premier academic institution in Uganda committed to developing well-rounded citizens who are prepared to face the challenges of the modern world with confidence, integrity, and excellence.

🔭 Vision

To produce Academically, Morally, Physically, Spiritually, Socially and Economically sound Citizens ready to meet the challenges of the modern World.

🎯 Mission

To be a center of excellence — fostering academic achievement, moral uprightness, and all-round development in every student who walks through our doors.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These pillars guide everything we do at Amus College School.

📖

Academic Excellence

We uphold the highest academic standards, equipping students with the knowledge and skills to excel in examinations and beyond.

🌟

Moral Integrity

Character development is central to our ethos. We model and instill honesty, respect, and ethical responsibility in all students.

💪

Physical Wellness

A healthy body supports a healthy mind. Our sports and physical education programs develop discipline and teamwork.

🙏

Spiritual Growth

We nurture the spiritual dimension of each student, building inner strength, purpose, and a connection to something greater.

🤝

Social Responsibility

Our students are taught to be compassionate community members who contribute positively to society and Uganda at large.

💼

Economic Empowerment

We equip students with entrepreneurial thinking and life skills that lead to self-sufficiency and economic independence.

World-Class Facilities

Built for World-Class Students

We are committed to providing our students with an environment where they can learn, train, and grow to their full potential.

⚽

Professional Football Turf

Our sporting infrastructure includes a professional-grade football turf — the same turf that hosted the 2024 FEASSA East African Games, along with netball, volleyball, and basketball pitches.

🏠

Girls' Dormitory — Complete

Our new girls' dormitory is complete and fully operational, providing comfortable and secure accommodation for our female students.

🏗️

Boys' Dormitory — Under Construction

Our new boys' dormitory is currently under construction and is expected to be completed by the end of 2025, further expanding our capacity to serve more students from across Uganda.
