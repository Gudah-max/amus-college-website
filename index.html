Academic Programs

Our comprehensive programs are designed to develop every dimension of a student's potential.

🎓

O-Level (UCE)

Uganda Certificate of Education program rigorous preparation ensuring students excel in national examinations and beyond.

🏆

A-Level (UACE)

Advanced-level studies guiding students to top university placements locally and internationally with focused academic pathways.

🎵

Music, Dance & Drama

Professional arts programs developing creativity, confidence, and cultural appreciation through performance and expression.

⚽

Sports & Athletics

Comprehensive sports program including football, netball, athletics, basketball fostering teamwork and physical wellness.

💻

ICT & Technology

Hands-on computer and technology education preparing students for the digital economy of the 21st century.

🏕️

Scouting

Building character, leadership, and community service values through structured scouting activities, outdoor adventures, and civic responsibility programs.

Life at Amus

Thriving in Every Way

Life at Amus College School extends well beyond the classroom and the pitch. We invest in the whole student — their health, their happiness, and their sense of community.

🍗

A School That Feeds You Well

Good nutrition is the foundation of good performance. At Amus, students enjoy chicken and rice every Wednesday, and meat and rice every weekend — wholesome, energising meals that fuel everything they do.

🎉

Faith, Unity & Celebration

We are a proudly inclusive school community. Our annual Eid celebrations bring together students of all faiths in a spirit of unity, joy, and shared experience — a tradition that reflects the warmth of Amus.

🙏

A Community Built on Faith

We acknowledge God's hand in all that we have achieved. From academic excellence to sporting triumphs and artistic brilliance, our journey has been one of faith, perseverance, and grace.
